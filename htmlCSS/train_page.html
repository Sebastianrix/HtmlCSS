Who doesn't wanna feel the power of a train at full speed? Trains rule the tracks — from old steam engines to futuristic bullet trains. While regular trains are built for comfort and cargo, high-speed trains push the limits of technology. The fastest bullet trains in the world, like the Japanese Shinkansen or French TGV, can reach speeds of over 320 km/h, with experimental maglev trains hitting 600 km/h!
