Flying through the skies at insane speeds — who wouldn't want that? Planes are not just for travel; they’re feats of engineering and speed. Commercial jets cruise at around 900 km/h, while military jets like the F-22 Raptor can break the sound barrier at speeds over 2,400 km/h. And if you're talking pure speed, the fastest manned plane ever, the SR-71 Blackbird, hit a mind-blowing 3,540 km/h!
